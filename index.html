What We Do & Why We Do

According to a recent analysis, 1.3 billion tons of food are allegedly wasted annually. This amounts to one-third of the entire amount of food generated for human use. When the sum is added up, 3 trillion meals are lost annually worldwide. Almost 9.8% of people worldwide are undernourished and have food cravings. Thus, we at NourishNet, a Cravingzzzz affiliate, have adopted that program to provide sustenance to people in need.
